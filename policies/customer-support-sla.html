{{organization.name}} Customer Support and SLA Policy

1.  Agreement Overview

This Agreement represents a Service Level Agreement (“SLA” or “Agreement”) between {{organization.name}} and its customers for professional services, software as a service, or any application support/service deliverable that was purchased from {{organization.name}} on a contract basis that includes a defined scope.

This Agreement remains valid until superseded by a revised agreement mutually endorsed by the stakeholders.  It does not supersede current processes and procedures unless explicitly stated herein.

2. Goals and Objectives

The purpose of this Agreement is to ensure that the proper elements and commitments are in place to provide consistent service support and delivery to our clients by {{organization.name}} and its affiliate providers.

The goal of this Agreement is to create a communication channel between services provisioned between {{organization.name}} and our customers.

The objectives of this Agreement are to:

  • Provide clear reference to service ownership, accountability, roles and/or responsibilities.
  • Present a clear, concise and measurable description of service provision to the customer.
  • Match perceptions of expected services provisioned with actual service support and delivery.

3.0 Service Agreement

The following service parameters are the responsibility of {{organization.name}}  in ongoing support of this Agreement. 

3.1 Service Scope

The following services are covered by this Agreement.

  • Monitoring e-mail and web support.
  • Remote assistance for our products (limited by service agreement) for fixes, troubleshooting, configuration, and maintenance.
  • Script and XSLT updates during software version upgrades.
  • Monitoring, health, and maintenance of SaaS infrastructure.
  • First line of support for third-party products sold by {{organization.name}}.

Services other than ones listed above are considered out of scope for support. For the avoidance of doubt, the following services are not considered support:

  • All onsite services including training, software installation and upgrades.
  • Consulting services.
  • Development of new scripts and XSL transforms including testing and project management of these activities.
  • Development of new InDesign templates and CSS including testing and project management of these activities.
  • Updating of scripts, XSL transforms, templates and CSS where the update is to add extra functionality or features and is outside of a software upgrade.

3.2 Customer requirements

Customer responsibilities and/or requirements in support of this Agreement include:

  • Payment for all support costs at the agreed interval.
  • Follow support procedures and use established reporting methods.
  • Request a change in ticket priority if deemed necessary.
  • Reasonable availability of customer representative(s) when resolving a service-related incident or request.
  • Prompt reporting of security concerns.
  • Eensure that the Customer’s computer systems are appropriate, adequately maintained and meet minimum technical standards determined by {{organization.name}}.

4.0 Service Management

Effective support of in-scope services is a result of maintaining consistent service levels. The following sections provide relevant details on service availability, monitoring of inscope services and related components.

4.1 Service Availability

Coverage parameters specific to the service(s) are customer business hours unless superseded by additional agreements. Support tickets received outside of office hours will be collected, however no action can be guaranteed until the next working day.

4.2 Service Requests

{{organization.name}} would provide ongoing support to customers using the approved service support channel and knowledge base resources. The time available is determined by the Ticket Priority and the SLA Policy for defined for each customer. 

Ticket Priority

{{organization.name}} assigns a priority to each ticket when it is received.  As per section 3.2, it is the customer's responsibility to request a change to this priority if they feel that a different priority level is required. For reference the following is a guide to how priority is determined:

  • Urgent: Critical production issue affecting all users, including system unavailability and data integrity issues with no workaround available.
  • High: Major functionality is impacted or performance is significantly degraded. Issue is persistent and affects many users. No reasonable workaround is available.
  • Normal: Intermittent system performance issue or bug impacting some workflows or users but not consistently. Short-term workaround is available, but not scalable.
  • Low: Inquiry regarding a routine technical issue; information requested on application, capabilities, navigation, installation or configuration; bug affecting a small number of users.

SLA Policy

For all support agreements {{organization.name}} uses Best Effort (BE) to respond during the stated response window.

  • Level 1 Support is not subject to a service level guarantee and is provided during the customer’s business hours (9:00am–5:00pm local time) only. {{organization.name}} uses their best efforts to respond to the support request within three business days.
  • Level 2 Support is a premium support level agreement backed by a service level guarantee. It is offered for normal business hours (9:00am–5:00pm local time) or on a 24x7 basis. Under Level 2 Support a support call is logged online, by email or by calling a dedicated phone number. Gold Support offers 4-hour call back and, if the issue is caused by a defect in the {organization.name}} software, {organization.name}}'s support team uses best efforts to remedy the defect or provide a workflow workaround within 10 working days. If it is a support query, {{organization.name}} responds with a detailed answer within one business day.
  • Level 3 Support is a premium support level agreement backed by a service level guarantee. It is offered for normal business hours (9:00am–5:00pm local time) or on a 24x7 basis. Under Level 3 Support a support call is logged online, by email or by calling a dedicated phone number. Platinum Support offers 2-hour call back and, if the issue is caused by a defect in the {organization.name}} software, {organization.name}}'s support team guarantee to remedy the defect or provide a workflow workaround within 10 working days. If it is a support query, {{organization.name}} responds with a detailed answer within one business day.

SLA Exclusions

After a report has been logged, {{organization.name}} responds in compliance with the relevant Service Level, unless the Customer does not comply with its obligations; or has engaged {organization.name}} to add functionality to the Software that significantly changes the architecture of the components of the Software or the structure of the XML file formats.
